How does "Speech-to-Launch" actually work?
You record a 2-minute thought. Picao uses multi-model intelligence to transcribe, identify core narrative arcs, and then refract that one thought into dozens of assets (LinkedIn, X, Substack, IG) tailored to each platform's unique algorithm.
What is the "Context Tax"?
The Context Tax is the 40% loss in productivity caused by switching between transcription apps, writing tools, and social schedulers. Picao is a unified workspace that keeps you in "Flow State" by housing the entire journey in one place.
